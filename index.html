Suspension on the Play Store

08/06/2018

Hey guys, some bad news.

Google recently took down Flappy Knuckles from the Play Store on the grounds that it violates their "spam" policy, even though the game has nothing to do with spam and doesn't violate any of their spam policies (I've checked).

I'm doing my best to get it back up, but if worst comes to the worst, I'll still have the PC version and I'll just have to upload an APK for the Android version and expect people to install it from that (which is tricky for younger players, as they probably won't know how to do it).

So, for now, it's not going to be up on the Play Store. I can't say for sure that it'll come back up, but it's a possibility and I really hope it gets unsuspended.

In the meantime, the PC version is still up on the GitHub and the itch.io page, so there's that. If the Play Store version doesn't get put back up, I'll upload the APK and put a link to that here. Sorry for the inconvenience, and stay hyped for Flappy Knuckles 2!
